Make Your Own Candles!

Candles are awesome to have around your house. They can make a room smell good and they give you a good feeling when you see them lit around the house, but buying them at a store can be a little scary. You don't know what is in them that makes them smell. Fake scents and perfumes can have negative effects on your health so we are going to show you how to make your own, all natural candles!

Gather Your Supplies

You will need:

  • wax - beeswax works great. You can also melt down old candles
  • candle wicks - get them at any craft store
  • dixie cups (wax cups) or glass jars
  • glitter! - because, why not!
  • a stovetop and something to melt the wax in

Step 1 - Melt the Wax

We use a saucepan filled half way with water, then a glass measuring cup with the wax inside. The water boils and heats up the wax. It works great! Make sure you have a parent with you when using the stove!

Step 2 - Add Glitter!

Glitter makes everything better, so pour it on! Just make sure you clean it up real well, because it will get everywhere! Mom was not happy! The glitter looks so cool in the wax and it also shimmers in the candlelight. so awesome!

Step 3 - Add the Wick

Now that the wax is melted, it's a good time to prep your container. Add the wick to the container and try to keep it in the middle.

Step 4 - Pour it On!

This is where you get creative. add coloring to the wax after you pour it in and mix it a bit. Let it cool for a few minutes, then add more wax, with different colors if you like. Repeat this until the candle is as big as you want or you fill up the container.

Step 5 - Let it Cool

Now that you have all the layers you want, just chill, for real! Let the candle cool for at least one hour. If you don't let it cool completely, it may get stuck to the wax cup when you peel it.

Step 6 - Peel the Cup

Time to reveal your masterpiece! Carefully peel the wax cup off of the candle. Look, it's beautiful! Good job!
